Golaž

Golaž is one of the best dishes in the world, in my humble opinion.

One of it's best attributes is that it's very simple to cook and I'll tell you how.

The recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Enogh tommato souce
  • Couple of laurel leaves
  • Spices

Notes about the ingredients:

  • Beef cuts are better the calf's, so please keep in mind while shoping.
  • Best beef cuts for golaž are shanks, from the side of the cow.
  • Quantity of the onions mainly influences how thick the stew is in the end. The golden ratio is said to be 1:1 meat to onions, but it's never followed except on some special occasions like birthday celebrations. to enhance thickness you can just add apple or flour.
  • Common ammount of meat is 1 kg
  • Common spices include salt, pepper, basil and whatever you heart desires. Personally I add cloves.

Instructions

  1. Dice onions and chop the beef into chunks.
    • The only important thing to take care of that all beef chunks are even. Best shape for that is a cube. The size only matters in terms of cooking time.
    • Smaller chunks cook faster.
  2. In pot roast onion in oil until it's nice golden colour.
    • Burned onion leave bitter taste.
  3. Add beef cubes. Grill on each side just a little.
    • Do not cook them through, as this step is only to allow chunks to keep shape in the final dish.
  4. Add tomato sauce and bring to boil.
  5. Add spices and add water until it's decently full pot.
  6. Cook on low fire for up to 4 hours.
    • Cooking time depends on a lot of factors, so my advice is just to try if the meat is cooked through and is really soft.
  7. Serve in bowls with bread.
